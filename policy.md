## Content & Purpose

This site publishes technical research on vulnerabilities, exploits, and malware observed in the wild. All content is written for the security community — defenders, researchers, and practitioners — to understand, detect, and respond to active threats.

No content on this site is intended to facilitate unauthorized access to systems, networks, or data.

## Educational Use & Disclaimer

Techniques, proof-of-concept code, and methodology described here are documented solely for **educational and defensive purposes**. Understanding how an attack works is a prerequisite for building effective defenses.

**Use of any technique, code, or method described on this site against systems you do not own or have explicit written authorization to test is illegal and unethical.** The author accepts no responsibility for misuse of information published here.

## Research Agent

The AI research assistant available on this site runs entirely within your browser session. It does not store your queries, conversation history, or any personal data. Requests are processed by Cloudflare Workers AI and are subject to [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Privacy

This site does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics beyond optional Cloudflare Web Analytics (aggregate traffic data only — no fingerprinting, no personal identifiers). No account registration or personal data collection occurs here.
